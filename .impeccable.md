## Design Context

### Users
ClipMark is for developer-friendly users who archive web content into local Markdown files for research, notes, references, and drafts. They use the app in a focused editing context where speed, predictability, and file ownership matter more than visual flourish.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel trustworthy, calm, and lightweight. It should read as a practical editor with strong usability rather than a decorative productivity suite.

### Aesthetic Direction
Favor a minimal light-mode interface with soft contrast, quiet surfaces, and restrained accents. Avoid eye-straining colors, loud saturation, or visual gimmicks. The experience does not need to mimic macOS closely, but it should still feel polished and native to a serious editing workflow.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize reading comfort and operational clarity over visual novelty.
2. Make structure obvious through spacing, typography, and surface contrast instead of heavy ornament.
3. Keep interactions calm and predictable, with clear focus states and low-noise feedback.
4. Let accent color guide action and state, but keep the overall palette neutral and non-fatiguing.
